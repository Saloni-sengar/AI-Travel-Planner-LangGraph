## 🗓️ Itinerary
Based on the provided information, I've created a 7-day travel itinerary for your trip to Mumbai:

**Day 1: Arrival and Gateway to Mumbai**

* Arrive at Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj International Airport (BOM)
* Check-in to your hotel (consider options like Holiday Inn Mumbai International Airport, Hotel Kohinoor Elite, or The Taj Mahal Tower)
* Visit the iconic Gateway of India
* Explore Colaba Causeway for shopping and street food
* Enjoy the evening at Marine Drive, also known as the "Queen's Necklace"

**Day 2: Mumbai's Cultural Heritage**

* Start the day at Kamala Nehru Park for a stunning view of the city
* Visit the Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Vastu Sangrahalaya (CSMVS) museum
* Explore the Haji Ali Dargah, a historic mosque and shrine
* Visit the Dhobi Ghat, a unique open-air laundry facility
* Enjoy traditional Indian cuisine at a local restaurant

**Day 3: Bollywood and Beaches**

* Take a guided tour of a Bollywood film studio (optional)
* Visit the Juhu Beach, a popular spot for street food and sunset views
* Explore the nearby Versova Beach and the Versova Fort
* Enjoy the evening at the famous Juhu Chowpatty, a street food heaven

**Day 4: Day Trip to Lonavala**

* Take a day trip to Lonavala (approximately 2 hours away from Mumbai)
* Visit the Bhushi Dam, a popular spot for trekking and picnics
* Explore the Karla Caves, a historic Buddhist site
* Enjoy the scenic views of the Western Ghats

**Day 5: Mumbai's Street Food and Markets**

* Explore the street food scene at the Mohammed Ali Road, known for its kebabs and falooda
* Visit the Crawford Market, a historic marketplace for fresh produce and souvenirs
* Explore the Chor Bazaar, a flea market for unique shopping experiences
* Enjoy the evening at the famous Leopold Cafe, a popular spot for food and drinks

**Day 6: Mumbai's Architecture and History**

* Visit the Victoria Terminus (CST) railway station, a UNESCO World Heritage Site
* Explore the St. John's Church, a historic church in the heart of the city
* Visit the Bombay High Court, a stunning example of Gothic Revival architecture
* Enjoy the evening at the Nariman Point, a popular spot for dining and nightlife

**Day 7: Departure**

* Spend the morning shopping for souvenirs or exploring a local market
* Depart for the airport and your onward journey
