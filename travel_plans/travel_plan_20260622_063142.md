**Day 2: Zurich to Lucerne**

* Take a train to Lucerne (approximately 1 hour journey)
* Visit the Chapel Bridge, Lion Monument, and Old Town
* Enjoy a scenic boat tour on Lake Lucerne
* Stay overnight in Lucerne

**Day 3: Lucerne to Interlaken**

* Take a train to Interlaken (approximately 2.5 hours journey)
* Visit the Höheweg promenade, Interlaken Monastery, and take a scenic boat tour on Lake Thun or Lake Brienz
* Enjoy outdoor activities like hiking, paragliding, or skiing (depending on the season)
* Stay overnight in Interlaken

**Day 4: Jungfraujoch and Lauterbrunnen**

* Take a train to Jungfraujoch, the "Top of Europe" (approximately 2.5 hours journey)
* Enjoy breathtaking views of the surrounding mountains and glaciers
* Visit the charming village of Lauterbrunnen and its picturesque valley
* Stay overnight in Interlaken

**Day 5: Adelboden and Bern**

* Take a train to Adelboden (approximately 2 hours journey)
* Visit the picturesque village and enjoy outdoor activities like hiking or skiing
* Take a train to Bern, the capital city of Switzerland (approximately 2 hours journey)
* Visit the Old Town, Zytglogge clock tower, and the Bundesplatz
* Stay overnight in Bern

**Day 6: Geneva**

* Take a train to Geneva (approximately 2 hours journey)
* Visit the Palais des Nations, St. Peter's Cathedral, and the Jet d'Eau fountain
* Enjoy a scenic boat tour on Lake Geneva
* Stay overnight in Geneva

**Day 7: Departure from Geneva**

* Spend the morning shopping for souvenirs or visiting any last-minute attractions
