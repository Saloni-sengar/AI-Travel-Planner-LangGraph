* Visit the Juhu Beach, a popular spot for locals and tourists alike
* Take a stroll along the Versova Beach, which offers a more secluded experience
* Explore the nearby Sanjay Gandhi National Park, which offers a variety of hiking trails and scenic views
* Enjoy a picnic lunch in the park
